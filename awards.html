CHAMPIONING EXCELLENCE: ST. ANTONY'S SCHOOL, GHAZIABAD

St. Antony's School, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, has set a new standard for educational excellence, earning the prestigious Education Excellence Empowering India Awards 2023. The school clinched the top spot among Private Unaided Schools in the state, a testament to its unwavering commitment to holistic education and overall development.

St. Antony's School achieved the pinnacle of educational distinction by securing the 'Education Excellence - Empowering India Awards 2023' for Private Unaided Schools in the state. This remarkable feat was attributed to a confluence of factors:

  • Outstanding Academic Performance: Consistent and outstanding academic results over the past years highlight the school's dedication to scholastic excellence.
  • Quality Leadership and Management: Distinguished governance model characterized by quality leadership and efficient management practices.
  • Co-Scholastic Excellence: Students showcased exceptional talent in co-scholastic areas, emphasizing a holistic approach to education.
  • Modern Infrastructure: State-of-the-art amenities that enrich the overall educational experience.
  • Highly Trained Staff: Dedicated and well-trained human resources ensuring the best possible guidance and education for students.
  • Health and Safety: Emphasis on health, hygiene, and safety measures, creating a safe and nurturing environment.
  • Culture of Innovation: Continuous introduction of new and creative ways to enhance the learning experience.

The award was received by Mr. Alok, Mr. Lijo, and Mr. Albin on behalf of Fr. Shinoj and St. Antony's School at the prestigious Award Distribution Ceremony held at Hotel The Claridges, New Delhi, on 8th February 2024. St. Antony's stands as a beacon of educational excellence, inspiring and empowering India's future leaders.

ABOUT THE SCHOOL

NEW DEPAUL SCHOOL Overview:

OUR PATRON ST. VINCENT DE PAUL New De Paul School, Berhampur, is named after St. Vincent De Paul, one of the pioneers of social reformation. The school had a humble beginning with 56 children and over the years it grew in size and popularity and has the strength of over 2800 students today.
